Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A simple business website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while e-commerce sites may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your pricing?

Our pricing includes custom design, responsive development, content management system, SEO optimization, and post-launch support. Additional features like e-commerce functionality or custom integrations may have separate pricing.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes! We offer maintenance packages to keep your website updated, secure, and running smoothly. This includes regular backups, security updates, content updates, and technical support.

Can you help with content creation?

While we focus on design and development, we can recommend trusted content writers and copywriters. We also provide guidance on content structure and SEO best practices.

What happens after the website is launched?

We provide training on how to use your content management system, deliver all source files, and offer ongoing support. We also monitor your site for the first month to ensure everything is working perfectly.
